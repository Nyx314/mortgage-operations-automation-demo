# Case Study

## Challenge

A mortgage operations team needed a more reliable way to prepare title and insurance requests while tracking incomplete information and vendor follow-ups.

## Previous process

Staff manually copied loan information, searched for lender clauses, assembled email requests, tracked responses, and maintained follow-up notes across separate tools.

## Implemented workflow

The demonstration centralizes operational records in Airtable, retrieves the appropriate clause according to order type, creates an approval-gated Gmail draft through Zapier, records the draft result, and surfaces actionable records through dedicated views and a daily digest.

## Controls introduced

- Manual review for missing clauses
- Draft-only outbound messages
- Test labeling
- Duplicate-draft prevention
- Explicit follow-up ownership
- Missing-item and vendor-ETA tracking
- Daily deadline visibility

## Evidence collected

Controlled testing verified title and insurance clause selection, title and insurance template generation, Gmail draft creation, Airtable writeback, duplicate protection, operational views, and scheduled digest delivery.

## Limitations

This public case study uses fictional information. Results from a live client pilot are not published. Client-specific lender requirements, insurance-document requirements, security policies, and compliance obligations require qualified review before deployment.
